Hogwarts

***Feel free to ask questions on school@gmail.com***

This is a school of magic with 4 different houses. Each house represents a different trait. Gryffindor house is full of brave people. Ravenclaw house is all about wit and intellegince. Slytherins are full of ambition and Hufflepuffs are loyal to friends. A magic hat called the sorting hat will place you in the hat that relates to you most. You will go on the Hogwarts Express. Hope to see you at Hogwarts!

Achievements

achievements in hogwarts

  • Albus Dumbledore

    Ablus Dumbledore is an accompished wizard. He fought against Gerrelt Grindlewald and Lord Voldemort. He has an order of Merlin, first class. This means that he has done many contributions to the Wizarding world.

  • Triwizard tournement

    Hogwarts has hosted the Triwizard tournement. The Triwizard tournement is a tournement that has three different schools fighting in contests to win glory for their school. The winner can get a lot of money.
